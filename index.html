About IUEAHRA

INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF EAST AFRICA (IUEA) Human Rights Association (IUEAHRA) was founded in 2014 after members realizing that the rights of Ugandans and most people in the communities around the world were not being given the respect accorded to them. It is built on principles of Democracy, Freedom and Progress to enhance the knowledge, respect and observance of human rights, promote exchange of information and best practices. And the members as Human Rights advocates are committed to building a better society characterized with the rule of law.

It exists as an independent, non-governmental, non-partisan and not-for profit human rights organization.

The association was created to serve IUEA students, humanity and its surrounding communities especially the under privileged, the disadvantaged and illiterates as we all work towards making the world a better place in terms of economic excellence, social welfare, peace, competitiveness, and integrity while conforming to the values of transparency, accountability, respect for human rights, social justice and transformation.

Vision

“To nurture and empower a Generation that values Human Rights as a platform for social welfare, peace, integrity, social justice, transformation and sustainable development in Uganda, Africa and globally.”
