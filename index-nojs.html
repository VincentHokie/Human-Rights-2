Welcome to the official International University of East Africa Human Rights Association website.
We are a network of over 60 students from various countries whos main target groups are students from primary and secondary schools, tertiary institutions, Universities and youths in Uganda generally with ambitions of spreading their reach to other African communities and other communities globally. We're determined and willing to be agents of change to our community.

Vision

“To nurture and empower a Generation that values Human Rights as a platform for social welfare, peace, integrity, social justice, transformation and sustainable development in Uganda, Africa and globally.”
